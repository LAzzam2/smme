Snail Mail My Email is a worldwide community art project where volunteers handwrite strangers' emails and send physical letters to the intended recipients, free of charge.

In 2014, 390 volunteers collectively sent 7,000+ letters over a five day span. A massive thanks to all who participated.

About

SMME was created in 2011 by artist
Ivan Cash, who originally conceived the project as a one-time, month long initiative where he would write and send out strangers’ emails one at a time.

The project ultimately received over 10,000 letter submissions and prompted Cash to enlist the help of hundreds of volunteers, sending letters all over the world.

SMME has since transitioned to a week-long annual event, held every November that anyone – even you – can participate in.
